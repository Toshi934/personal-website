About Me

I am a PhD student in Public Health with a deep interest in uncovering how familial relationships impact health outcomes. My research bridges epidemiology and sociology by applying rigorous causal inference methods.

My primary methodological focus is on leveraging state-of-the-art causal inference techniques to draw robust insights from observational data.
